Nestled among towering pines on the shores of Lake Joseph, the Sherwood Inn provides guests an authentic Muskoka experience. We have secured exclusive use of the resort for the weekend and hope you will consider staying with us as we celebrate our marriage.

Amenities

Canoes, Kayaks, Pedal Boats, Paddle Boards Private Beach, Hot Tub, Towel Service Free Round of Golf at Lake Joseph Club Tennis, Fitness Room, Fat Bikes, Hiking Croquet, Washoes, Shuffle Board, Table Tennis, Darts

Spa

This on-site spa offers services such as massages, nails, facials, body scrubs and wraps, and express treatments. For more information and to schedule your appointment ahead of time, please visit their website.

Booking

Cost is $199/night. Your one or two-night accomodation includes:

  • Exclusive access to resort amenities listed above
  • Full breakfast buffet in the Main Inn (Vintages Dining Room)
  • Free Round of Golf and shuttle service to Lake Joseph Club

For room information please visit the Sherwood Inn's website. To book a room for the wedding you must contact Sherwood Inn by phone and specify you are a guest of the Keys wedding.

When filling out the RSVP you will have an opportunity for special requests regarding your room choice. Your room type and location is decided by the wedding couple, but your request is appreciated and will be taken into consideration. If you need more information about room options, please feel free to contact the Sherwood Inn or the bride and groom.
